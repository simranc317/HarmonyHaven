Diet Tips

Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables.

Choose whole grains over refined grains.

Include lean protein sources in your meals.

Avoid sugary drinks and snacks.

Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Sample Meal Plan

Here's an example of a healthy daily meal plan:

Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and a glass of orange juice

Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette dressing

Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and almonds

Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed vegetables

Here's another example of a healthy daily meal plan:

Breakfast: Whole wheat toast with avocado and poached eggs

Lunch: Quinoa salad with black beans, corn, and salsa

Snack: Apple slices with peanut butter

Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with vegetables and brown rice

Here's a third example of a healthy daily meal plan:

Breakfast: Smoothie made with spinach, banana, almond milk, and protein powder

Lunch: Turkey wrap with whole grain tortilla, lettuce, tomato, and mustard

Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks

Dinner: Grilled shrimp skewers with quinoa and roasted vegetables
